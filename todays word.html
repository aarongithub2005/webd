King Solomon in this chapter tries to tell us about how God decides our fate if we trust in him and if we commit our plans to him. Humans generally jump into conclusions about their lives and do not put their trust in God. We are easily discouraged by the fact that our life isnt going well or if we are facing many trials and problems. For these kind of people this verse from Proverbs chapter 16 really comes in need. It is not in our hands to decide our fate and our future but its in God's hand. So let us put our trust in him and be happy because he has carved us in the palm of his hand and cares for us. Trusting him in hard times can be really difficult, but its the best thing we can do.
